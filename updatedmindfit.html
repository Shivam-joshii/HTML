ABOUT

MindFit is a health and fitness tracking application designed to help users maintain a healthy balance between their physical and mental well-being. The application allows users to track important daily activities such as exercise, sleep, water intake, and diet. By monitoring these habits, MindFit encourages users to develop a healthier lifestyle and better understand how physical health and mental health are interconnected. Its goal is to motivate individuals to stay consistent with their wellness routines and improve their overall quality of life.

How It Helps Users

  • Tracks their Daily sleep.
  • Tracks their daily water intake.
  • Tracks daily workout.
  • Moniters mood.
  • Giveinsights based on sleep , exercise , mood and water intake.
